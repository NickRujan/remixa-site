Privacy Policy

Short version: your videos never leave your machine, and we don't track you. Here's the whole story.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Your videos stay on your computer

All video processing happens locally. Your clips are never uploaded to us or anyone else — there is no server-side rendering, no cloud storage, no copy of your footage anywhere but your own machine.

The only network calls the app makes

  • License verification — the app sends your license key, a device identifier, and receives your activation status. This is what keeps your subscription working.
  • Emoji fetching (optional) — if your text uses emoji, the app downloads the emoji images it needs for rendering.
  • Update check — the auto-updater asks whether a newer version exists.

That's the complete list. None of these calls carry your videos or their contents.

Purchase data

When you buy Remixa, your name, email, and payment details are collected and processed by our payment processor — not stored by us. What we see is order and subscription status tied to license keys, so we can activate and support your license.

No tracking. No ads. Nothing sold.

  • No analytics or tracking in the app or on this site.
  • No ads, anywhere.
  • No data sold, ever, to anyone.
