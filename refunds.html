Refund Policy

All sales are final, with the specific exceptions spelled out below.

Last updated: July 2026

1. The general rule: all sales are final

Remixa delivers its full value the moment you buy: your license is active immediately and video generation is unlimited from the first minute. Because a billing period you've had access to can't be handed back, we do not refund purchases or renewals — including for change of mind, time you didn't use, or the results your videos get on third-party platforms. The only exceptions are the ones listed on this page.

Prices are $99/month or $990/year, in US dollars (USD). Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, our merchant of record — Remixa never sees or stores your card details.

2. Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel any time from the Lemon Squeezy billing portal (linked in your purchase receipt). Your plan stays active until the end of the period you've paid for, then simply doesn't renew.

  • Cancelling does not refund charges already made, and unused time is not pro-rated.
  • Renewal charges are non-refundable. It's your responsibility to cancel before your renewal date if you don't want the next period.

3. First purchase: your 14-day withdrawal window

If this is your first-ever Remixa subscription, you can request a full refund within 14 days of the charge — provided you've generated fewer than 20 videos since that charge.

By purchasing, you expressly request immediate access to Remixa and acknowledge that once you've generated 20 or more videos, you give up this withdrawal right to the maximum extent your local law allows. Where your local law preserves a withdrawal right even after use, any refund is reduced proportionately to reflect the days elapsed and your usage.

This window applies to the first purchase only — never to renewals or plan changes.

4. Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits consumer rights that can't be waived — including in the EU, UK, and Australia. If Remixa is faulty as a digital service and we can't fix it after you report it, you're entitled to the remedies your local law provides; where the law strictly requires a refund, we comply.

If something isn't working, tell us first — most problems are fixable, usually quickly.

5. Billing errors

Genuine billing mistakes are always corrected: if you're charged twice for the same period, or charged after you completed a cancellation, we'll put it right through Lemon Squeezy.

6. Chargebacks

Contact us before disputing a charge — billing errors and statutory refunds are handled without any fight. A card dispute filed to get around this policy results in suspension of the license and forfeiture of the remaining subscription period.

7. How to request a refund

Email billing@remixa.app from the email address you purchased with, and include your receipt. Approved refunds go back to the original payment method via Lemon Squeezy.

If this policy changes, the new version applies to charges made after the update.
