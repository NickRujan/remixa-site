FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

How many videos can I make?+
As many as you want — there are no credits and no monthly cap. The more clips you add, the more ways Remixa can mix and match them, and you can generate up to 1,000 videos in a single batch.
Do I need to split my clips up first?+
No. Drop in whole recordings. Auto mode detects your clip's structure — where the talking ends and the screen or reaction begins — and repurposes it for you.
Will platforms flag them as duplicates?+
Each output is built from a different combination of your clips and wordings, then gets subtle automatic variation — color, temperature, zoom, and speed. You control how strong that variation is.
Where do my videos render?+
On your own computer. Nothing uploads to a server — your footage never leaves your machine.
Do I need editing skills?+
No. You drop in your clips, set your text once if you want, and hit Generate.
What do I need to run it?+
A Windows or Mac computer. Installation is one click — no plugins, no accounts, no editing software.
What format are the outputs?+
Vertical 1080×1920 (9:16) — the standard shape for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.
