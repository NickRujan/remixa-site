Getting started

Setup · 2 minutes

Install and activate

Windows

  1. Download Remixa-Setup.exe, double-click, click through.
  2. Open Remixa from the Start menu.

Mac

  1. Drag Remixa into Applications.
  2. First launch: right-click → Open, confirm once.

Paste the license key from your receipt email. One key, 2 devices.

No key yet? Grab Remixa from the pricing page. Your key arrives in your receipt email right after purchase.
Step 1

Drop in whole clips

No cutting, no modes to pick. Auto reads each clip's structure and builds the right videos. It handles all of these:

Mix clips: Off = one clip per video · Hooks = openers rotate · All = mix everything. Up to 1,000 videos per batch.

Step 2

Add your text

Text shows on your hook, or the Snapchat style runs the whole video. Give a text block several wordings and each video picks one.

Happy with it? Save it as a layout and reuse it on any batch.

Step 3

Overlays

Drop in a logo or sticker. One click removes its background. Text always renders on top.

Step 4

Music

Slide the wave to set where each song starts. Then set three things, independently, per sound:

  • Ducking: duck under your talking (default), or leave it off.
  • Playback: play over the whole video (default), or fade in after the hook.
  • Volume: 0 to 200% per sound, applied before ducking.
Step 5

Auto-variation

Every copy gets small zoom, color and speed tweaks, so no two videos look the same. Adjust or switch off in Settings.

Step 6

Generate & download

One button. Outputs land as remixa1, remixa2, … Grab them all with Download all.
